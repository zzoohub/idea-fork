report_problem Problem Statement

Freelancers struggle to separate personal and business expenses automatically, leading to hours of manual reconciliation during tax season. Existing tools are often too complex (Quickbooks) or too simple (Excel), lacking intelligent automation for "solopreneur" workflows. Users express frustration with categorization fatigue and fear of missing deductions.

1

AI-First Receipt Parsing & Classification

Develop a mobile-first interface that uses advanced OCR and LLMs to not just read the total, but understand the context of the purchase (e.g., "client dinner" vs "personal meal") based on location and time. [1] [3]

2

"Tinder for Expenses" Workflow

Gamify the reconciliation process. Connect bank feeds and allow users to swipe left (personal) or right (business) on transactions. Batch processing for recurring payments. [2]

3

Real-time Tax Liability Estimation

Provide a running tally of estimated taxes owed based on classified income and expenses, removing the end-of-year surprise factor.
